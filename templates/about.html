About the App

AI Student Helper is a modern educational assistant built to support students across all academic subjects. Whether you're struggling with complex mathematics, writing essays, understanding historical events, or learning a new programming language — our AI is here to guide you every step of the way.

Powered by cutting-edge AI models including Groq Llama 3 and Google Gemini, the app delivers fast, accurate, and contextually relevant answers tailored to your learning needs. Every response is designed to not just answer your question, but help you truly understand the concept.

Built with simplicity and focus in mind, AI Student Helper keeps distractions to a minimum so you can concentrate on what matters most — learning and growing.

Lightning Fast

AI Student Helper is incredibly fast. Groq's high-speed Llama 3 answers your questions in fractions of a second so you never have to wait.

Multi-Subject AI

Struggling with Math? Science? Computer Science? History? English? Our advanced AI has been trained on all academic subjects.

Chat History

Your questions and the AI's step-by-step answers are automatically saved to your private History page so you can study them anytime.
